Class Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to develop academic skills, creativity, and critical thinking from Kindergarten to Grade 12.

  • Kindergarten: Play-based learning, basic numeracy, storytelling
  • Primary (1-5): English, Math, Science, Art, Sports
  • Middle (6-8): Literature, Algebra, General Science, History
  • High School (9-12): Subject specialization, labs, career prep

Exam Information

We conduct assessments throughout the year to ensure steady progress:

  • Unit Tests - Monthly
  • Mid-Term Exams - September
  • Annual Exams - March
